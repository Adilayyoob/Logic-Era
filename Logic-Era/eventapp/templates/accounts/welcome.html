Farookian event management portal helps you to create and join events happening at Farook College.
To create or join an event first, register yourself to the Farookian event management portal and log in with your registered account.
You will be redirected to the Home page, where you can see upcoming events and statistics.
To create an event go to the "Create" page in the navbar and Enter the details.
To Join an event go to the "Join" page in the navbar.
